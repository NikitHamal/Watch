Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in the watch industry:

2020

The Beginning

Chetana's Watch Store was founded with a vision to make premium timepieces accessible to watch enthusiasts in Nepal. Started as a small online store with a curated selection of quality watches.

2021

First Milestone

Reached our first 1,000 customers and established partnerships with renowned watch brands. Launched our customer service center to provide better support.

2022

Expansion

Opened our first physical showroom in Kathmandu and expanded our team. Introduced watch repair and maintenance services.

2023

Digital Innovation

Launched our advanced e-commerce platform with enhanced features. Introduced virtual try-on technology and expanded to international shipping.

2024

Recognition

Received "Best E-commerce Platform" award and reached 10,000+ satisfied customers. Launched our smartwatch collection and sustainability initiatives.

2025

Future Vision

Continuing to innovate with AI-powered recommendations, expanded product lines, and enhanced customer experience. Planning regional expansion across South Asia.
